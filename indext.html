Golden / 골든

누가 들어도 Golden임을 알 수 있을 만큼의 독보적인 음색을 가진 H1GHR MUSIC의 보컬리스트 Golden은 원래 G.Soul이라는 이름으로 오랜 시간 활동하다가, 2017년 H1GHR MUSIC에 입단했다.


2019년 군 제대 후 황금 같은 음악을 하는 아티스트가 되겠다는 다짐의 ‘Golden’이라는 새로운 이름을 내세워 EP [Hate Everything(2019)]을 발매하며 본격적인 활동을 시작했다


이전에 EP [Circles(2017)], [Dirty(2015)], [Coming Home(2015)]를 발매하였으며, ‘I`ll Be There(2017)’, ‘술버릇(bad habit)(2017)’, ‘Tequila(2017)’, ‘멀리멀리(2016)’, ‘Smooth Operator(2016)’, ‘Love Me Again(2015)’, ‘You (Acoustic Ver.)(2015)’ 등의 싱글과, ‘Better (Feat. Golden)-WOOGIE(2020)’, ‘NO WAY (Feat. G.Soul)-이하이(2019)’, ‘Wine (Feat. G.Soul)-주영(2018)’, ’15 (Feat. G.Soul)-pH-1(2017)’, ‘ANTI (Feat. G.Soul)-지코(2017)’ 등의 곡에 참여하며 본인의 목소리를 계속해서 알렸다.


최근에 Golden은 <보이스 코리아 2020>에서 우승을 거머쥐며, 대중들에게 더 강력하게 자신의 이름을 각인했다


자신만의 사운드와 가사에 집중할 수 있는 음악을 하고자 하는 뮤지션인 그는 지금도 완벽한 보컬을 위해 꾸준히 연구하고 연습한다고 하는 노력형 천재인 Golden의 황금 같은 목소리로 들려줄 이야기가 기다려진다.


H1GHR MUSIC vocalist Golden possesses a unique sound which anyone can distinguish. Golden originally used the name G.Soul for quite a long time and joined H1GHR MUSIC in 2017.


After finishing his military service in 2019, with making golden and timeless music in mind, he started his full-fledged music career with his EP [Hate Everything(2019)] under the new name ‘Golden’.


In the past Golden released EPs [Circles(2017)], [Dirty(2015)] and [Coming Home(2015)], singles ‘I’ll Be There(2017)’, ‘bad habit(2017)’, ‘Tequila(2017)’, ‘Far, far away(2016)’, ‘Smooth Operator(2016)’, ‘Love Me Again(2015)’ and ‘You (Acoustic Ver.)(2015)’, and collaborated with other artists in ‘Better (Feat. Golden)-WOOGIE(2020)’, ‘NO WAY (Feat. G.Soul)-LEE HI(2019)’, ‘Wine (Feat. G.Soul)-Jooyoung(2018)’, ‘15 (Feat. G.Soul)-pH-1(2017)’, ‘ANTI (Feat. G.Soul)-ZICO(2017)’


Particularly, Sik-K was invited by Virgil Abloh to decorate the show as a runway model for the 20/21 FW Fashion Week "TORNADO WARNING" collection. It was an unusual event for a Korean artist to be on the Paris Fashion Week stage, and Sik-K proved his status globally as a trend setter of not only music but also the fashion industry


Recently Golden won first place in Voice Korea 2020 and made his name widely known amongst the public. As a musician who wants to make music that focuses on his own sound and lyrics, Golden is an endeavoring genius who constantly researches and practices for perfect vocals. We look forward to the story Golden tells with his golden voice.
